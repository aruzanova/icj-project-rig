Yellow-billed cuckoo

Scientific Name: Coccyzus americanus

Taxon: Birds

Description: In Texas, the Western Distinct Population Segment of this species breeds in riparian woodlands below 6,000 feet in the Trans Pecos region, arriving in May and wintering in South America. Threats include hydrologic changes preventing cottonwood and willow regeneration and livestock impacts on sapling trees.
